About Us

“Millions saw the apple fall, but Newton asked WHY .” Curiosity makes humans different from animals. It's this curiosity which changed the way we travel, eat and live. Skillonation is an Holistic Development, based skilling platform which believes in imbibing this curiosity into the minds of your young readers , so that education becomes an act of curiosity.

It's our mission and vision at Skillonation to provide kids with courses which will not only enhance their left brain of logic and analysis but would also enhance their right brain of creativity , arts and visualization which is a predominant factor in all the innovation we see today.
Our teaching methodology includes a more practical approach, where we teach kids to create their own projects to build their analytical and craftsmanship skills by creating their pathway through problems and searching for their own solutions.These courses are custom-built for students under K - 12 for their individual needs.
